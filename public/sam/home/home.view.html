Welcome to SAM - Scenario Assessment Model!

SAM is a tool that measures the performance of Watershed MVP scenarios related to Cost, Community, and Confidence. Overall, SAM aims to assess whether a scenario is financially viable, socially aligned, and future oriented compared to other possible scenarios that could be generated.
